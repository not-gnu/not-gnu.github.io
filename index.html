the gum tree

"Readers beware, my emotions ebb and flow like changing waves on these pages: sometimes shallow and soft, filled with light; sometimes high and crashing, thick and darkness. Inconsistency is far away. In the process, I'm being told several things, like a religion I'm forced to believe but simply don't. Henceforth, I write a minestrone of an ode, a eulogy, a lament, a rant, and a... ballad.

As these words appear on my screen, I am acutely aware that this is an epilogue to chapters of many. Each one is a self-driven journey to unravel more of the tangled web of my thoughts and experiences, as unpredictable and varied as the tides. This journey through love, loss, and self-discovery is far from over."
