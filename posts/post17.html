Intro written 31 Aug: Today is the last day of winter. Soon the first day of spring.

Days will slowly get hotter and longer. Perennial flowers will soon bloom. Magpies will then attack me and Kirby during our walks.

I’m hoping (again) that this won’t be my last winter. Hopefully, I won’t spend it alone again.

This winter is probably the best I’ve had so far. I’ve met some amazing people, started doing some extra work, and have made some progress with my projects. I’ve also reunited with Kirby who’s arguably the only one bringing some joy (and stress) to my life.

I want to log most of my experiences during this season. The first & second part is a series of photos, while the third is a journal log entry.

I want it to be very casual because I'm so busy and preoccupied that I don't want to take non-work stuff too seriously.
